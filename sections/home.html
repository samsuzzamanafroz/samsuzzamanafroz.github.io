Samsuzzaman Afroz

PhD Researcher| Gravitational-Wave Astrophysics and Cosmology

My research focuses on fundamental physics, astrophysics, and cosmology using gravitational-wave observations. I work on cosmological parameter estimation and tests of gravity in both the weak- and strong-field regimes, as well as the astrophysical population of compact binary objects, particularly binary black holes. My research combines gravitational-wave data from current and future detectors-LIGO–Virgo–KAGRA, LISA, Cosmic Explorer, and the Einstein Telescope, with cosmological probes such as DESI and Pantheon supernova data.
